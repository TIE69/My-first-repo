Motive of Explore with Dahuk

DAHUK is for the backpackers where we can share and collect information about traveling around Bangladesh. This group is non–profitable & non–commercial where our principle desire is to Share our thoughts or testify of what we have experienced and witnessed. May our testimony inspired others to share their story.
